About

Taghazout Surf Expo 2025 is an international sports and tourism event held on the beaches of Taghazout, Morocco. It brings together surf enthusiasts, professional athletes, global brands, and visitors from around the world. The expo aims to promote surf culture, boost coastal tourism, and create a unique space for cultural exchange. Over three days, participants will enjoy sports activities, training workshops, live music performances, and a vibrant festival atmosphere by the Atlantic Ocean.
